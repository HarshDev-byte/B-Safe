# Privacy Policy for B-Safe

**Last Updated: January 3, 2026**

## Introduction

B-Safe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Emergency Contacts**: Names, phone numbers, and email addresses of people you designate as emergency contacts
- **Profile Information**: Your name, blood group, medical conditions, and allergies (optional)
- **Account Information**: Email address when you sign in with Google

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Location Data**: GPS coordinates during active SOS emergencies only
- **Device Information**: Device model and OS version for crash reporting
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous app usage patterns to improve the service

## How We Use Your Information

| Data Type | Purpose | Shared With |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Emergency Contacts | Send SOS alerts | Your designated contacts only |
| Location | Include in emergency alerts | Your designated contacts only |
| Medical Info | Include in emergency alerts | Your designated contacts only |
| Analytics | Improve app performance | Firebase (anonymized) |

## Data Storage

### Local Storage (On Your Device)
- Emergency contacts
- SOS history
- User settings
- Audio recordings (if enabled)

**We do NOT store your personal data on external servers.** All sensitive information remains on your device.

### Cloud Storage (Optional)
If you sign in with Google, the following may be synced:
- Emergency contacts (encrypted)
- Basic settings

## Data Sharing

We **DO NOT**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with advertisers
- Track your location when SOS is not active
- Access your contacts without permission

We **DO** share data with:
- **Your Emergency Contacts**: During SOS events only
- **Firebase**: Anonymous crash reports and analytics
- **Google**: Authentication services (if you use Google Sign-In)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all data stored in the app
- **Delete**: Remove all your data by uninstalling the app
- **Export**: Contact us to request a copy of your data
- **Opt-out**: Disable analytics in app settings

## Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Encrypted local storage
- Secure HTTPS connections
- No plain-text password storage
- ProGuard code obfuscation

## Children's Privacy

B-Safe is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
